Strength

The strength of a basketball team not only depends on the performance of players in the team but also the situation of the team includes salary structure and the degree of running-in of the team. In our project, we will combine and consider all mentioned factors.

Top 100 Players Number and Winning Rate

We collect the winning-percentage data of the current team in 2020 and the top 100 players' data, then integrate them into the new data. As Figure1, the X-axis is the winning percentage of a team, and the Y-axis is the number of 100 players on a team. If the number of players in the top 100 is higher, then the winning percentage is directly related to the number of good players.







However, we found that it's not as simple as putting all the top 100 players in a single team to improve the team's winning percentage. So we analyze that some players can bring better scores to the team, while others can't.So we've focused on two top players(James and Cousins) and the benefits they bring to their team.



Cousins

Cousins is an American professional basketball player. Nicknamed "Boogie", he played college basketball for the Kentucky Wildcats, where he was an All-American in 2010. He left Kentucky after one season, and was selected with the fifth overall pick in the 2010 NBA draft by the Sacramento Kings. In his first season with the Kings, Cousins was named to the NBA All-Rookie First Team, and from 2015 to 2018, he was named an NBA All-Star.He is also a two-time gold medal winner.





James

LeBron Raymone James Sr. is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Widely considered one of the greatest NBA players, James is frequently compared to Michael Jordan in debates over the greatest basketball player of all time.Playing on the Cleveland Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and Los Angeles Lakers, James is the only player in NBA history to have brought NBA championships to three franchises as Finals MVP.





We can judge that the top players can drive a team, but not all players can promote the team's victory, because the position and ability of players are different, the motivation of the team is also different. An all-around player like James can really affect a NBA team's play.
