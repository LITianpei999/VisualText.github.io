Strength


The strength of a basketball team not only depends on the performance of players in the team but also the situation of the team includes salary structure and the degree of running-in of the team. In our project, we will combine and consider all mentioned factors.



Guess and Discussion

Guess: More good players in a team, the winning percentage of this team is better.

Oservation: It's not as simple as putting all the top 100 players in a single team to improve the winning percentage of a team.



Example: Houston doesn't have a winning percentage as high as L.A.Lakers despite having four top players.








There are also differences in the combination of different players and different teams. This means that not the addition of outstanding players can improve the team's performance. We use Cousins and James as examples.

It is believed that a higher salary can Inspire commitment. In this part, we will prove this point by visualization, and find the relationship between performance and salary.



Purpose: Analyze which factors are related to salary.

Analysis Object: MPG; PPG; APG and so on.

Evaluation Index: Covariance.

Conclusion: The visualized results confirm that there is indeed a correlation.

Common Situation: The high salary probability of a player is related to the player's ranking.

Fitness



Some passive elements (such as fatigue) influence the performance of the team, which even leads to the failure of strong teams. We want to analyze whether these objective factors will have an impact on the team's game







In general, the more injured the team, the lower the winning rate of the whole team.

From the comparison between the two figures, the more games played per month, the more injuries.









We can see the relationship between the home and away win rates of each team over time from the above figure.

We can also see the historical ranking changes of the team and judge whether the team performance is stable.



Geography



We suspect that there is a geographical factor affecting the morale of the team. For example, the home team doesn't have to travel far to play, and players don't miss games because they're afraid of heights or tired. Or because of the convenient transportation, the audience watching the game greatly inspires the team and causes the team to perform well.





Most of the blue points locate in the developed area, which has developed traffic network.



Developed traffic network means the audience more convenient to come to the field.



More audience can make the team have higher morale.







We use the Gephi to visualize all the team according to their win-loss relationship.



Gephi uses modularity algorithm help us find potential communities. We can see that it related to the geography.



We mark the birthplaces of the top 100 players on the map to visualize which areas can produce more outstanding players.
